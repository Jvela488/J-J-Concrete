About J&J Concrete

Built on hard work and local trust.

We are a family‑owned concrete contractor based in Thomasville, NC, focused on honest communication, dependable schedules, and long‑lasting work our customers are proud of.

Trusted across the Piedmont Triad

J&J Concrete Specialists Inc. was founded to bring professional project management to concrete work of every size, from small backyard patios to multi‑phase commercial pours.

Our crews understand local soils, freeze‑thaw conditions, and the demands placed on concrete surfaces in high‑traffic environments. We carefully plan each project for proper base prep, drainage, reinforcement, and expansion to control cracking.

When you hire J&J, you work directly with decision‑makers who live and work here. We show up when we say we will, protect surrounding landscaping, and leave your property clean.
